QUICK START
1. Upload and Activate the On Demand Theme Within Your WordPress Admin.
2. Activate the Required "Options Framework" Plugin.
3. Click "Appearance" Followed by "Theme Options" and then "Save Options".
4. Configure the "Theme Options" including the "Content Options" Tab And The "Customization Options" Tab. (IMPORTANT)
